Top Stories
Defense

Takaichi cabinet approves record FY2027 defense budget request

The cabinet endorsed an ¥8.7 trillion defense request, up about 9% year-on-year, prioritizing standoff missiles, munitions stockpiles, and Nansei-island air defense. The Finance Ministry will finalize the budget in December.

So what: Sets up a December Diet fight over the funding source as the temporary tax measures from the 2022 buildup plan expire.

Pattern: Continues the trajectory set by the Dec 2022 National Security Strategy's 2%-of-GDP target.

Reuters · Tokyo
Economy/BOJ

BOJ holds rate at 0.50%, trims growth outlook

The Policy Board voted unanimously to keep the short-term rate at 0.50% and lowered its FY2026 GDP forecast to 0.9%. Governor Ueda said the bank would move cautiously while watching wage momentum and yen-driven import prices.

So what: Pushes market expectations for the next hike toward autumn, keeping pressure on USD/JPY near 152.

Bloomberg · Tokyo
China-Japan

Tokyo protests sustained China Coast Guard presence near Senkakus

MOFA summoned a senior Chinese diplomat after eight CCG vessels operated in the contiguous zone and two entered territorial waters. The Joint Staff also reported a JASDF scramble against a PLA reconnaissance drone over the East China Sea.

So what: Adds friction ahead of a planned foreign-ministerial contact later this month.

Pattern: Echoes the elevated patrol cadence seen since the 2012 nationalization of the islands.
